# ecommerce pack

This initial local pack uses the packaged `candidate-ranker-v3` artifact and conservative
ranker-local thresholds.

It is intended for product detail pages and simple ecommerce-like cards. It does not claim
coverage for arbitrary storefronts, personalized pages, checkout flows, or anti-bot protected
sites. Abstention is expected when the page contains multiple plausible prices or ad/recommendation
regions without enough evidence.
